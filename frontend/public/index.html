What is LifeVault?

LifeVault is your free & secure and easy online journal. We'll send you an email every day and you simply reply with what you did for the day and your journal is saved. We'll also send you random reminders from the past with every daily email. You'll love reliving the memories you'd long forgotten. This makes your life better. What's the catch? There's none. This is my gift to humanity.

Just Hit Reply

We send you an email every day and you just hit reply. It really is that easy. Forget all those journals you tried to keep before but failed. This one will work for you.

Your Journal is Encrypted.

I don't what anyone reading my journal so we store all journals encrypted with your password. Even with direct access to the database, we can't read your journal. And you have my promise that we'll keep the database secure and never access your data. You also have my promise that if I ever shut this down, you'll get 6 months notice and will be able to download a simple backup of all your data.

Free as in Air.

I'm giving this out, completely free to the world. Some day I may add some optional premium features to help pay for the service but at the moment, for at least the next 2 years, I'll be maintaining and paying for this out of my own pocket. The world needs this. Thank me by helping blow this up - tweet about it etc.
