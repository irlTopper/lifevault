Who are you and why did you make this?

Thanks for asking. I'm Peter Coppinger, an Irish man and CEO of Teamwork.com

Everyone wants to keep a journal, right? But if you're anything like me you've tried and failed several times before.

I found this fantastic service called OhLife that allowed me to keep a journal simply by replying to a daily email. It was free and it was fantastic.

When they released a paid plan, I even paid for it as a thank-you dispite not needing the extra features.

Sadly, in late 2014, OhLife shut down. I, and many others were bitterly disappointed.

I've been journal-less for months. Nothing else seemed to have the simplicity I wanted.

And so... I decided to make this simple service and give it out completely free for as long as I can.

In addition I'm going to give the assurance that I always wanted from OhLife - I store all the journal entries encrypted and I will never read your journal. That's a promise.

I'll keep this free for as long as I can and if I ever have to shut it down you'll get at least 3 months of warning and be able to download all your journal.

So please sign-up and enjoy.

Why did you make LifeVault? I used to love this free journal service called OhLife! Unfortunately they shut down and I was beyond disappointed. I think this world could benefit from this and I'm going to do my best to provide it for free forever. I hope you love it.
